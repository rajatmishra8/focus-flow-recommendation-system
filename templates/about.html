What is Focus-Flow?

A smarter way to study. Built by students, for students.

The Problem We're Solving

You open your phone to check one notification. Thirty minutes later, you're still scrolling. Sound familiar? Today's students are fighting a constant battle between their goals and their screens. Stress, distraction, and digital addiction are quietly destroying academic performance — and nobody is talking about it.

What Focus-Flow Does

Focus-Flow is your personal AI-powered study assistant. It asks you a few questions about how you're feeling, analyzes your patterns using machine learning and natural language processing, and gives you a clear, actionable plan to study smarter — not harder.

Think of it like a smart coach that learns from you over time and gives advice that actually fits your situation.

Machine Learning

Our AI model was trained on real student data to predict your performance and understand what's holding you back.

NLP Understanding

Describe your problems in plain English. Our system reads your words and understands if you're stressed, distracted, or burned out.

Adaptive Learning

Every piece of feedback you give improves future recommendations. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

Built-in Tools

Integrated Pomodoro timer, focus tips, and study planning guides — everything you need in one place.

How It Helps You

  • 🟢 Tells you exactly what's blocking your focus — whether it's stress, screen time, or poor sleep
  • 🟢 Gives you a step-by-step personal plan — not generic advice, but tailored to your situation
  • 🟢 Tracks your progress over time so you can see yourself improving
  • 🟢 Explains why it makes each recommendation — no black boxes
  • 🟢 Helps you build better habits around studying and screen use

The Science Behind It

Focus-Flow is built on research in behavioral psychology, cognitive science, and educational technology. The Pomodoro Technique was developed by Francesco Cirillo and has been validated by countless productivity researchers. Our ML model uses a Random Forest classifier trained on behavioral surveys to identify patterns that predict academic performance.

TextBlob sentiment analysis processes your written feedback in real-time, allowing the system to detect emotional tone and continuously improve its recommendations.
